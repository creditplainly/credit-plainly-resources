# Glossaries

Glossaries help you learn the vocabulary used on credit reports and in scoring discussions. They are study aids, not a substitute for verifying what appears on your own reports.

## Credit Plainly glossaries

- Credit report terms glossary: https://www.creditplainly.com/resources/credit-report-terms-glossary
- Credit score terms glossary: https://www.creditplainly.com/resources/credit-score-terms-glossary

## How to use these pages

Use a glossary when you see an unfamiliar term. Understanding a term does not by itself prove an error, a legal violation, or a particular dispute outcome.
