# Tools and checklists

These are public Credit Plainly tools intended for education and organization. They do not submit disputes for you and do not guarantee results.

## Tools

- Dispute letter generator: https://www.creditplainly.com/tools/dispute-letter-generator
- Credit utilization calculator: https://www.creditplainly.com/tools/credit-utilization-calculator
- Credit score scenario estimator (educational): https://www.creditplainly.com/tools/credit-score-scenario-estimator

## Checklists

- Collection dispute checklist: https://www.creditplainly.com/tools/collection-dispute-checklist
