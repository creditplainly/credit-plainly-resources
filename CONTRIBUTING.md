# Contributing

Thank you for helping keep these reference lists accurate.

## What is welcome

- Broken link reports (please use the issue template when possible)
- Suggestions for additional **official** government or program pages that fit the topic

## What is not welcome

- Affiliate links, partner promotions, or paid placements
- Pitches for lenders, credit cards, credit repair services, or other commercial products
- Legal advice requests or personalized dispute coaching in pull requests

## How to report a broken link

Open an issue using `.github/ISSUE_TEMPLATE/broken-link.yml` and include the URL, where you found it, and (if you have one) a verified replacement.

For broader suggestions, keep the focus on **public official sources** and neutral educational context.
